INGLÊS COM ANICETO

    LESSON 01: GREETINGS

    Greetings (Saudações)

    Good morning ( Bom dia)
    Good afternoon (Boa tarde)
    Good Evening (Boa noite)

    NOTE: "Good night" (Boa noite) não se usa para cumprimentar mas sim para desejar uma boa noite ou despedida.

    1- How are you?- I am fine.
    2- How are you doing? - I am doing well/fine/great/good.
    3- How is it? - Good/ okay/Cool.
    Querendo saber de volta pergunte...
    1- And you?
    2- How about you?

    Dialogue 1 (formal)
    John: Hello, Good morning!
    Jack: Good morning. How are you?
    John: I am fine, thank you.
    Jack: Welcome.

    Dialogue 2 (informal)
    John: Hi, how are you doing?
    Frank: Hi, Doing great. How about you?
    John: Good too. Thanks.

    New words
    1- morning /mónin/
    2- afternoon /aftanún/
    3- night /naitth/
    4- good / güd/
    5- how /hau/
    6- thank / thank/( th= S pronunciado com a língua entre os dentes)
    7- hi /hài/
